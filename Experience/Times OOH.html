Times OOH is India's leading Out-of-Home advertising company, managing premium advertising inventory across airports, metro, street furniture and other key transit environments.

The company specializes in creating impactful OOH experiences that connect brands with their target audiences across high-traffic premium locations. Times OOH operates advertising solutions across major airports in India, including Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata, and Bengaluru, helping brands maximize visibility among high-value audiences.

Competitive Analysis

Key Findings & Recommendations

My research uncovered several important insights about the Kolkata airport advertising market:

Market Potential Findings

  • Sector Analysis: Real Estate emerged as the priority sector with the highest advertising potential, followed by Food & Beverages, while underrated sectors included Travel-Tourism and Health
  • Client Demographics: 34-41 was identified as the most preferred target demographic among clients, with more clients preferring arrival zones over departure areas
  • Market Penetration: Extrapolation of the research data indicated that approximately 50% of local clients in the database had suitable potential for airport advertising
  • Market Expansion: Many local brands that had achieved national status were focusing on expansion outside Kolkata, creating new geographic targeting opportunities

Challenge Identification

  • Price Perception: Local clients had a perception of high pricing that needed to be countered with value-oriented communication
  • Target Audience Misconceptions: Many clients incorrectly believed their target audience was not present at the airport
  • Innovation Perception: Clients perceived the Kolkata airport as not being sufficiently innovative compared to other metro airports
  • Digital Concerns: Mental blocks existed regarding dwell time for Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) properties
  • Comparison Bias: Subconscious comparisons to Mumbai and Delhi airports created unfavorable perceptions that needed addressing

Strategic Recommendations

  • Market Education: Create awareness campaigns highlighting that Kolkata airport advertising costs approximately 1/3 of Mumbai and Delhi rates
  • Case Studies: Develop and promote success stories in pitch decks and through various media channels
  • Data-Driven Approach: Release data about DOOH dwell times to correct client misperceptions
  • Premium Client Strategy: Create a specialized program with special offers for high-potential clients
  • Traffic Analysis: Implement seasonal analysis to target clients based on airport traffic patterns
  • Expanded Inventory: Explore innovative spaces like aerobridges for advertising opportunities
