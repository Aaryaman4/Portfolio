Company Overview

Lenskart is transforming the eyewear industry in India, shifting the perception of glasses from mere utility to a fashion statement and style accessory.

Founded in 2010, Lenskart has grown to become India's largest eyewear company with both online and offline presence across the country. Through a combination of technology innovation, direct-to-consumer business model, and focus on quality, Lenskart has revolutionized how Indians purchase eyewear. The company aims to position eyewear as a coveted fashion accessory, leveraging product excellence, AI insights, and a social-first marketing strategy to appeal particularly to urban youth.

Role & Responsibilities

As a Research Intern at Lenskart, I was part of a strategic initiative to redefine eyewear as a fashion statement for urban youth. I conducted comprehensive market research using multiple methodologies to decode fashion trends and consumer behavior around eyewear.

Primary Research

Conducted 21 in-depth interviews and 2 focus group discussions with 18-25 year olds in Tier-1 cities to understand perceptions of style, fashion, and eyewear.

Secondary Research

Analyzed social media trends across platforms to identify emerging fashion patterns and eyewear preferences among target demographics.

Semiotic Analysis

Applied semiotics to decode cultural representations of style and eyewear, identifying key cultural codes that connect eyewear to fashion identity.

Campaign Development

Developed strategic campaign recommendations based on research insights to position Lenskart eyewear as fashion accessories.

Key Insights

Through our research, we uncovered several important insights about how consumers perceive and interact with eyewear:

  • Eyewear lets you choose who you want to be - Glasses are seen as a tool for expressing different personas and identities
  • Relationship with eyewear is complicated - Many consumers experience tension between choice (fashion) and chance (necessity)
  • The essence lies in the details - Small elements like eyewear make a significant impact on overall style perception
  • Styling specs with ethnics is a challenge - Many consumers struggle to incorporate eyewear with traditional Indian clothing
  • It's all about the fit - How eyewear complements facial features is crucial to consumer satisfaction
  • Fashion is cyclical and therapeutic - Dressing up, including eyewear choices, serves as both self-expression and emotional release
  • Confidence is rare - There's an opportunity to position eyewear as a confidence-boosting accessory
