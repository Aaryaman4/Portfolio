Company Overview

The Baker's Dozen is a premium artisanal bakery brand focused on authentic, preservative-free bread and bakery products, with a strong emphasis on traditional baking methods and high-quality ingredients.

Founded in 2013, The Baker's Dozen has grown to become one of India's leading artisanal bakery brands, with a presence across major cities through both retail partnerships and e-commerce channels. The company prides itself on maintaining traditional baking methods at scale while continuously innovating its product range to meet evolving consumer preferences.

Role & Responsibilities

As Assistant Marketing Manager, I was responsible for leading multiple aspects of the brand's marketing strategy and execution, with a particular focus on digital campaigns, brand awareness initiatives, and consumer research.

Campaign Management

Developed and executed data-driven marketing campaigns across digital platforms, with a focus on optimizing reach, engagement, and conversion metrics.

Consumer Research

Designed and conducted comprehensive NPS studies and consumer research initiatives to gather actionable insights for brand strategy refinement.

Stakeholder Management

Coordinated cross-functional collaboration between creative, media, and sales teams to ensure integrated marketing efforts and consistent brand messaging.

Content Strategy

Developed and implemented a founder-centric content strategy across social media platforms to position the brand as an industry thought leader and drive engagement.

  • Grew social media following by 25% MoM and increased organic engagement by 50% across multiple platforms by developing and implementing a founder-centric content strategy that positioned the brand as an industry thought leader
  • Improved brand awareness by 15% based on insights from 450 respondents across three major cities by designing and conducting comprehensive NPS studies and implementing targeted refinements to brand strategy
