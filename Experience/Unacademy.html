Company Overview

Unacademy is one of India's largest learning platforms, focused on revolutionizing education through technology and making quality learning accessible to students across the country.

Founded in 2015, Unacademy has grown from a YouTube channel to becoming one of India's leading ed-tech companies. With a vast network of educators and a comprehensive range of courses for various competitive examinations, Unacademy serves millions of learners through its subscription-based model and free content across digital platforms.

Role & Responsibilities

As a Management Trainee in the Brands team, I was responsible for executing strategic marketing initiatives across multiple education verticals, with a particular focus on live events, influencer collaborations, and revenue-driving campaigns.

Event Management

Stakeholder Management
