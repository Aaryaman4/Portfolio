About Me

Aaryaman Gadhavi

Marketing professional specializing in brand strategy, consumer insights, and digital campaign execution.

With a passion for understanding consumer behavior and transforming insights into impactful marketing strategies, I've built my career on creating data-driven campaigns that deliver measurable results. My experience spans across diverse sectors, from e-commerce to education technology, where I've consistently leveraged analytical thinking and creative problem-solving to drive business growth.

At The Baker's Dozen, I led initiatives that resulted in significant increases in brand awareness, engagement, and sales through innovative packaging design, targeted social media campaigns, and strategic partnerships. Prior to that, at Unacademy, I executed large-scale educational events that generated substantial revenue and viewership through cross-functional coordination and stakeholder management.

My approach to marketing combines rigorous research methodologies with creative storytelling, ensuring that campaigns not only reach the right audience but also resonate with them on a deeper level. I believe in the power of authentic brand narratives and data-informed decision-making to create meaningful connections with consumers.
